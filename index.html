The Recognition of Self Interpretation


What is art?

  • There are various ways to distinguish and define art. According to Britannica.com , art, also called visual art, a visual object or experience consciously created through an expression of skill or imagination.

  • Another definition of art is, art is the expression or application of human creative skills and imagination, typically in visual form such as painting or sculpture, producing works to be appreciated primarily for their beauty or emotional power.






What is music?

According to Merriam-Webster.com Music is the art of producing pleasing or expressive combinations of tones especially with melodies, rythms, and usually harmonies.




What are the benefits of Art and Music in schools?

Some benefits provided by ArtandMusicCenter.com , of both art and music include:

  • Music boost your brains, music is proved to stimulate a part of your brain associated with academical achivements, such as reading and mathematics, and emotional development.

  • Art improves academic achievements, in schools, students involved in arts have tremendous academic benefits compared to students without exposure to arts. It helps students increase motivation, attitudes, attendance and academic performance.

  • Art and Music can also enhsnce:

    • creativity skills

    • social skills

    • language and reading skills

    • critical thinking skills

    • self confidense
